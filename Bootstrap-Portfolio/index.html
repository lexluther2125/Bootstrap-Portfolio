About Me

Hi there!

My name is Lexi Majoue and I am passionate about researching how things work in the world around me. I love to apply the answers I find into improving upon whatever it is that I've learned. From fixing the AC in my car to fiddling and tweaking with the HTML code of a personal blog (long before I delved into professional coding), I find great joy and excitment in learning, creating, and problem-solving.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy scouting out where the best tacos are, spending time outdoors, catching live music in downtown Dallas, and supporting local artists. I look forward to creating a rich portfolio of my projects and sharing them with you here!
